How to Play

You can use your mouse and your keboard to interact with this app.

To move to the next or previous card you can click on the navigation arrows on the screen or press the left arrow key for previous and the right arrow key for next.

To flip the card (show the answer) you can click the "Show Answer" button or press the down arrow key.

To mark the card as correct you can click anywhere on the card or you can press the up arrow key.

To restart the deck and reset the scores click on the "Reset Deck" button.
